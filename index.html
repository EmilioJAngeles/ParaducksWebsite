Paraducks

About

Paraducks is a first-person puzzle game where you must create and coordinate
time loops, working with youself from the past to solve puzzles in the future!
But there's a catch - you must not allow your past iterations see you, as that
could create a paradox that threatens to unravel all of time and space.
Oh yeah, and you're two ducks piloting a mech suit!
